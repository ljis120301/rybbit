Access organization settings by clicking your profile icon and selecting "Organization Settings".

![organization settings](/docs/organization-settings.png)

## Organization

### Organization Name

Update your organization's display name. Only the organization owner can change this setting.

### Delete Organization

Permanently delete the organization and all associated data including sites, analytics, reports, and member associations.

<Callout type="warn">
  This action cannot be undone. You must type the organization name to confirm deletion. If you have an active subscription, you must cancel it before deleting the organization.
</Callout>

## Members

View and manage all members in your organization. The members table shows:
- **Name** - Member's display name
- **Email** - Member's email address
- **Role** - Owner, Admin, or Member
- **Site Access** - Which sites the member can access
- **Joined** - When they joined the organization

### Member Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to all features, can manage members, billing, and delete the organization |
| **Admin** | Can manage members and access all sites, cannot delete the organization or manage billing |
| **Member** | Can view analytics for assigned sites only, cannot manage settings or other members |

### Inviting Members

Owners can invite new members or create user accounts. See the [Inviting Users](/docs/inviting-users) guide for detailed instructions.
